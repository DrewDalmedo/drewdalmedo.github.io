*"One who is everywhere is nowhere. Those who travel all the time find that they have many places to stay, but no friendships...
A wound does not close up when one is always trying different dressings on it; a seedling that is transplanted repeatedly
will never grow strong." - **Seneca, 'Letters on Ethics To Lucilius'***

There is a tendency, especially amongst ambitious and inexperienced developers, to pursue the "latest and greatest" frameworks, languages, and tools to develop applications with.
The intent is for these developers to gain the edge over their contemporaries, and get in "on the ground floor" as it were. 

This pursuit is misguided.
